# InterviewMate: Unified Credit Business Model & Strategy Plan

## 1. Executive Summary
**InterviewMate** is an AI-powered SaaS platform providing highly realistic mock interviews, group discussions, and career tools (Resume and LinkedIn analysis). By deploying our proprietary "Vapi-Free" custom AI engine, we have decoupled from expensive AI middleware. This allows us to offer hyper-accessible pricing with industry-leading 95%+ profit margins under a flat-rate **Unified Credit Model**.

## 2. Value Proposition
* **All-in-One Prep Ecosystem:** Mock Interviews, Group Discussions, and Resume/LinkedIn Builders in one place.
* **On-Demand Practice:** Candidates can practice highly specific scenarios whenever they want.
* **Friction-Free "Unified Credits":** 1 Credit = ₹1. No complex mental math for users. 
* **Flat-Rate Transparency:** Simple, predictable credit costs for every feature.
* **Actionable Feedback:** Instant, detailed analytics on performance and communication skills.
* **Shareable Proof of Competence:** Custom result cards (LinkedIn-ready) that signal a candidate's preparedness to recruiters.

## 3. Target Audience
* **Primary (B2C):** Active job seekers, university students, and fresh graduates aiming for top-tier placements.
* **Secondary (B2B):** Bootcamps and staffing agencies wanting scalable interview prep for their cohorts.

## 4. Pricing Plans: The "Flexi-Credit" Strategy
InterviewMate operates on a "Flexi-Credit" model where exactly **1 Credit = ₹1**. Pricing is designed to capture massive market share while retaining insane gross margins.

### A. The "Burnout" Free Tier (Lead Generation)
* **Cost:** ₹0
* **Includes:** 30 Credits
* **The "Goldilocks" Burnout Path:** A user spends 10 Credits on a Mock Interview, 8 Credits on a GD, 5 Credits on Resume Analysis, and 5 Credits on the LinkedIn Optimizer. They are left with exactly 2 credits—not enough for another core session. After experiencing the "Aha!" moment across the entire platform, they hit the paywall and are heavily incentivized to upgrade.
* **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Offset:** Providing this generous free experience costs the company mere pennies in API overhead internally.
